Our Founder

Tiffany Joy Murchison

Founder & Principal Public Relations Strategist, TJM & Co. Media Boutique

Tiffany Joy Murchison is a consummate communications professional and an influential entrepreneur leading a boutique public relations firm.

As the founder and principal public relations strategist for TJM & Co., Tiffany Joy leads a team in providing bespoke public relations and brand development solutions for nonprofit, faith-based, and community engagement organizations, as well as small businesses and collegiate level sports organizations. Her unique strategies engage external and internal audiences by telling the story of a company’s core purpose in a way that preserves its truth and value. TJM & Co.’s client roster includes the New York City Council, Bedford Stuyvesant Restoration Corporation, and the One Hundred Black Men of America Association, to name a few.

Tiffany Joy’s experience spans several industries, from communications, technology, and financial services to publishing and entertainment. She has over 20 years experience in nonprofit board management. She is a regular public and media relations contributor for local television, workshop facilitator, and youth mentor. Her resume also boasts a stint as a radio talk show co-host.

Tiffany Joy holds a BA from The City College of New York, a Project Management Certificate from Villanova University, and Business Certificate in Women’s Entrepreneurship from Cornell University.

Our Purpose

TJM & Co. Media Boutique is a full-service public relations and brand management agency specializing in strategies for nonprofit, faith-based, and community engagement organizations, small business, politics, and sports. We know that public relations strategies work best from the inside out so we carefully study your internal circumstance and your external position. Your purpose becomes our passion. Thus, we are able to design a blueprint for telling your story and conveying your message without forgoing value or integrity.

Our Approach

We are absolutely certain that a good reputation is the most important thing for any brand. We work with you to ensure that both your reputation and your actions will provide your brand with longevity.

We have both monthly retainer and project-based offerings, providing precisely the level of service you need.

Our Difference

PR WORKS BEST FROM THE INSIDE OUT

Dressing up a brand that is struggling is like putting lipstick on a pig. It’s not pretty and still a pig. Our team possesses the expertise to provide counsel on organization culture. Thus, we assure that what we promote is the true character of your brand. We study your external and internal audiences to ensure that your brand shines brightly from any angle.

YOUR PURPOSE IS OUR PASSION

At TJM & Co. Media Boutique, we are particular about our clientele. We will only work with a brand whose purpose we can truly stand behind. Nor will we commit to more work than our team can handle without sacrificing excellence. Our clients experience a level of intimacy not blurred by layers management. We become our clients partners and their purpose becomes our passion.

SMALL AGENCY, BIG RETURN ON INVESTMENT

The bottom line is that because we are a smaller agency we can serve our clients by offering lower retainer fees than our larger counterparts. Yet, we deliver an excellent quality of service.
